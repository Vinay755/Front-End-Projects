Steve Jobs

1955 - 2011

Early Life

Steve Jobs was born on February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California, and was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.

Career at Apple

Steve co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976 with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne. He played a key role in the development of iconic products like the Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad.

Achievements

Steve Jobs was known for his innovation and design sense. His contributions to technology revolutionized various industries.
